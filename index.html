Building Communities Through Innovation

AICODEV Ghana (Alternative Innovations For Community Development) is a non-profit organization based in Wa, Upper West Region, Ghana, focused on community development and research.

About Us

Who We Are

AICODEV Ghana is dedicated to supporting deprived communities through health education, volunteer training, and social advocacy.

Our Mission & Vision

AICODEV Ghana works 'to Change lives and build communities to have equal access to all Resources and social justice'.

A world of equal rights and access to all services for all.

Compassionate Aid

We deliver assistance with dignity and respect for all people.

Community Partnership

We work with local communities to develop sustainable solutions.

Transparent Impact

We measure and report on the outcomes of all our programs.

Global Reach

Our programs span across the Wa West District in the Upper West Region of Ghana.

Thematic Areas

HEALTH

Focuses on enhancing well-being through sustainable, equitable systems, covering disease prevention, health service delivery, and workforce development.

HUMAN RIGHTS

Involves the universal protection, promotion, and implementation of fundamental freedoms, focusing on dignity, equality, and justice.

Poverty Eradication & Livelihoods Empowerment

Aimed at shifting vulnerable households from dependency to self-reliance through capacity building, asset accumulation, and financial inclusion.

EDUCATION

Focuses on improving access to quality education, promoting literacy, and ensuring inclusive learning opportunities for all.

WATER AND SANITATION

Aiming for universal access to safe drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) in communities.

NUTRITION

Focuses on improving nutritional outcomes, especially for children and mothers, through targeted interventions and community-based programs.

Our Projects

We implement sustainable projects that create lasting impact in communities.

Ongoing

Social Behaviour Change Communication to prevent Malaria in pregnancy

Increasing IPTp uptake by influencing knowledge, attitudes, and social norms among pregnant women, families, and health workers.

Completed

Healthy Mothers, Smiling Babies Campaign

Addressing maternal and newborn mortality through high-quality care that supports women physically and emotionally before, during, and after birth.

Ongoing

Increasing COVID-19 Vaccine Uptake in Ghana

Community engagement and education initiatives to increase vaccination rates through awareness campaigns and addressing vaccine hesitancy.

Ongoing

Increasing Immunization Coverage in Children

Community advocacy and mobilization to improve immunization rates for children under five years through outreach and education.
